How This Actually Works

So here's the thing - every website you visit can see your IP address. That's just how the internet works. When you click a link, your browser talks to the website's server, and the server can see where the request came from. Our tool just creates a special link that records this information before sending the person to wherever you want them to go. We also grab browser info (like what phone or computer they're using) because browsers send that automatically. It's not magic or hacking - it's literally what every website does. We just make it available to you. 


		$user_ip = $_SERVER['REMOTE_ADDR'];
		var user_agent = navigator.userAgent;

How IP Tracking Works

Our system works by creating a unique redirection link that passes through our servers before sending the visitor to the destination URL. During this quick redirection, we collect and analyze visitor data including:

  • IP address and geolocation
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device
  • Screen resolution and language
  • Referral source

Technical Information

IPLogger works by utilizing common web technologies to collect visitor information. When someone clicks your tracking link, our servers capture available data through standard browser APIs and server-side technologies.

Server-side IP collection:

$user_ip = $_SERVER['REMOTE_ADDR'];

Browser information collection:

var user_agent = navigator.userAgent;

This information allows our system to generate detailed reports on visitor activity, enabling you to analyze traffic patterns and understand your audience better. All tracking is performed anonymously and data is securely stored.
