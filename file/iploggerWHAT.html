What is IPLogger?

Understand how IPLogger works and what it can do for you

Overview

IPLogger is a versatile tool that allows you to track and analyze the traffic through your links, visitors on your online store, blog, or website.

With IPLogger, you can find out the IP address, location, device, browser, and other information of your visitors. You can also check URLs for hidden redirects and safety reasons, making it a valuable tool for both analytics and security purposes.

Our service works by creating a short link that contains a tracking code. When someone clicks on your short link, IPLogger collects and stores their data on a dashboard that you can access anytime. You can also create other types of loggers, such as an invisible pixel, a location tracker, or a mobile phone tracker.

What IPLogger Can Help You With

IPLogger can help you with various purposes across different domains:

Marketing

Measure the effectiveness of your campaigns, optimize your landing pages, and target your audience better with detailed visitor insights.

Security

Detect suspicious activity, prevent fraud, and protect your online assets by monitoring who accesses your links and content.

Research

Gather data for your projects, surveys, and experiments to better understand user behavior and geographic distribution.

Personal Use

Use for harmless fun interactions with friends, or to confirm if someone has viewed content you've shared with them.
