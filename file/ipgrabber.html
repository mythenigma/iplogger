What is an IP Grabber?

An IP Grabber is a tool that allows you to create specialized links that collect information about the people who click on them.

When someone clicks on a link created with our IP Grabber tool, you can log their IP address, location, device type, operating system, browser details, and more. This information is stored securely on our servers and made available to you through an easy-to-use dashboard.

IPLogger's IP Grabber tool is designed to be simple to use yet powerful enough for professional applications in marketing, research, security, and more.

Information You Can Track

Our IP Grabber tool collects a comprehensive set of data points about your visitors:

IP Address

The unique identifier assigned to the visitor's device on the internet.

Geolocation

Approximate country, city, and region of the visitor based on their IP address.

Internet Service Provider (ISP)

The company providing internet access to the visitor.

Device Type

Whether they're using a desktop, mobile phone, tablet, or other device.

Operating System

The OS running on the visitor's device (Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, etc.).

Browser Information

The web browser and version they're using to access your link.

Time of Visit

The exact time and date when the visitor clicked on your link.

Referrer URL

The website or platform where they found and clicked your link (when available).

How IP Grabber Works

Our IP Grabber service uses a redirect mechanism to collect visitor data. Here's how it works behind the scenes:

1

Create a Tracking Link

You enter the destination URL (where you want visitors to end up) into our system.

2

Get Your Custom Short Link

Our system generates a unique, shortened link that contains tracking code.

3

Share Your Link

You share this custom link with your audience through any channel - email, social media, messaging apps, etc.

4

Data Collection

When someone clicks the link, they briefly pass through our servers where we collect their information.

5

Seamless Redirect

The visitor is quickly and seamlessly redirected to the original destination URL.

6

Access Your Data

You can view the collected data in real-time through your IPLogger dashboard.

Pro Tip: The entire process is invisible to the visitor - they simply see that they've been taken to the target website. The redirect happens so quickly that most users won't even notice it.

Privacy & Ethical Usage

While our IP Grabber tool is powerful, we encourage all users to use it responsibly and ethically. Here are some guidelines:

Acceptable Uses
  • Analytics for your own marketing campaigns
  • Protecting your digital content
  • Academic research with proper consent
  • Tracking engagement with content you've shared
  • Verifying access to your shared resources
Unacceptable Uses
  • Stalking or harassing individuals
  • Collecting data for illegal activities
  • Attempting to gain unauthorized access to systems
  • Misleading people about the nature of the link
  • Any activity that violates privacy laws
Legal Note: Always ensure your use of this tool complies with all applicable laws, including privacy regulations such as GDPR in Europe and CCPA in California.
