Introduction

IPLogger is a powerful web service that provides a simple and free way to track IP addresses of online visitors. It offers a range of tools that allow users to create custom links that can be used to log the IP addresses of anyone who clicks on them. These links can be sent via email, chat, or social media, and when the recipient clicks on the link, their IP address is recorded along with additional information such as:

  • Geographic location (country, city)
  • Device type (mobile or desktop)
  • Operating system and version
  • Browser information
  • Referral source

One of the key features of IPLogger is the URL redirect functionality, which allows users to create custom links that redirect to a specified URL after the IP address is logged. This feature is useful for marketing campaigns, website traffic monitoring, and various security purposes.

Step 1: Access the IPLogger Homepage

Visit the IPLogger website homepage where you'll find the URL tracking tool at the top of the page.

Step 2: Create Your Tracking Link

Enter the destination URL that you want visitors to be redirected to after their IP is logged. This could be any website, social media page, or document link. After entering the URL, click the "Make it TRACKABLE" button to generate your tracking link.

Step 3: Get Your Tracking Links

After generating your link, you'll receive two important URLs:

  1. New Tracking Link: This is the link you'll share with others. When they click it, they'll be redirected to your specified URL while their IP is logged.
  2. Results Page Link: This is your private link to view the tracking results and visitor information.

Step 4: View and Analyze Results

When someone clicks your tracking link, visit your Results Page to see detailed information about the visitor. You can view their IP address, location, device information, and more. You can also:

  • Click "Show Details" to view expanded location information
  • Copy the IP address for further research
  • Delete records you no longer need
  • Edit your redirect URL if needed

Common Use Cases

Email Marketing

Track campaign performance by monitoring how many people click on your links and where those clicks originate from. This helps optimize campaigns and improve conversion rates.

Traffic Monitoring

Create custom links for different channels to track where your website traffic is coming from and optimize your marketing strategies.
