IPLogger or Grabify?

I've used both. Here's which one I think is better (and why)

People ask me this all the time - "should I use IPLogger or Grabify?" I've used both for the past year, so here's my actual experience with them.

Quick Answer

For most people: IPLogger
It's simpler, has no account requirement, and does everything the free version of Grabify does but without the annoying parts.

But if you need custom domains: Grabify
Grabify has more custom domain options if that matters to you.

The Actual Differences

IPLogger

Good stuff:

  • No sign up required (huge win for me)
  • Links never expire - created a link 6 months ago, still works
  • Way less ads than Grabify
  • Has email tracking built in
  • Actually free - no premium tier trying to upsell you

Not so good:

  • Fewer custom domain choices
  • Interface is basic (but I don't really care about fancy UI)

My take: This is what I use now. It just works, no fuss.

Grabify

Good stuff:

  • Lots of custom domain options (like bmwforum.co, stopify.co, etc.)
  • Been around forever, so it's reliable
  • More advanced analytics if you make an account

Not so good:

  • ADS. SO. MANY. ADS. Seriously, it's annoying
  • Free links expire after 30 days
  • Some features need account creation
  • Constantly pushing you to upgrade to premium

My take: It works fine, but the ads got on my nerves after a while.

When to Use Which

Use IPLogger if:
  • You just want something simple that works
  • You don't want to create an account
  • You might need email tracking too
  • You hate excessive ads
  • You're doing a quick one-time thing
Use Grabify if:
  • You really want specific custom domains
  • You're already used to Grabify
  • You have a premium account
  • Ads don't bother you

My Personal Experience

I started with Grabify because that's what everyone talked about. Used it for maybe 3-4 months. It worked fine, but the ads started getting annoying. Plus having my links expire was frustrating - I'd send someone a link, they'd click it a month later, and it wouldn't work anymore.

Switched to IPLogger when I found out it doesn't require signup. Honestly haven't looked back. Does everything I need without the annoyances.

That said, Grabify isn't bad. It's just that for what I personally need (simple IP tracking), IPLogger is cleaner.

Bottom Line

Both work. IPLogger is simpler and more straightforward. Grabify has more customization but more hassle. Pick based on what matters more to you - simplicity or customization.
