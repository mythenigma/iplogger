What is IPLogger?

IPLogger is a comprehensive online tool designed to help users track and log the IP addresses of visitors. Unlike complicated analytics platforms, IPLogger focuses on simplicity and effectiveness, providing you with the exact information you need without the complexity.

Whether you're a content creator wanting to know where your audience is coming from, a marketer tracking campaign effectiveness, or a developer needing to debug geographic-specific issues, IPLogger provides a streamlined solution to capture visitor information in real-time.

The tool works by generating custom tracking links that, when clicked, record information about the visitor including their IP address, geographic location, device type, operating system, and browser details.

The IPLogger dashboard provides an intuitive interface for tracking and analyzing visitor data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about IPLogger

Is using IPLogger legal?
Yes, using IPLogger is legal in most jurisdictions. However, it's important to use it ethically and in compliance with local privacy laws. In some regions, you may need to disclose to users that you're collecting their IP address information. Always check your local regulations regarding data collection and privacy.
How accurate is the geolocation data?
IPLogger's geolocation data is highly accurate at the country level (99%+) and generally accurate at the city level (80-90%). Various factors can affect accuracy including the use of VPNs, proxy servers, or mobile networks. For most standard use cases, the accuracy is more than sufficient.
Does IPLogger work with all types of links?
Yes, IPLogger works with virtually any destination URL. You can track links to websites, social media profiles, documents, images, videos, and more. The tracking occurs before the user is redirected to the final destination.
How long is the visitor data stored?
With the free plan, visitor data is stored for 30 days. Premium plans offer extended storage options up to unlimited retention, depending on the subscription level. You can always export your data at any time for permanent storage.
Can visitors detect that they're being tracked?
The tracking process is designed to be seamless and undetectable to the average user. The redirect happens very quickly, and there are no visible indicators that tracking is taking place. However, technically savvy users might notice the redirect process.
