Our Service

We appreciate your interest in IPLogger, a comprehensive web service that allows you to log and track IP addresses of anyone who clicks on your links.

IPLogger is more than just a URL shortener—it's a full analytics platform for your link traffic. Our service provides:

Custom Links

Create shortened links that track visitor data while redirecting to your target URL.

Location Tracking

See where your visitors are coming from with detailed location information.

Device Analytics

Collect information about visitor devices, browsers, and operating systems.

Traffic Insights

Analyze visitor patterns and track engagement with your shared links.
