IPLogger for Beginners

First time using IP tracking? Start here

Never used IPLogger before? No problem. I'll walk you through everything. By the end of this, you'll be able to track someone's IP like a pro. (Spoiler: it's easier than you think)

Step 0: What You Need

Requirements:

  • A web browser (you're using one right now)
  • A way to send links (text, email, Discord, whatever)
  • That's literally it

What you DON'T need: Account, payment, technical skills, software installation

Your First Tracking Link (Step by Step)

1. Open IPLogger

Go to iplogger.icu in your browser. You'll see a simple page with an input box.

Notice something? No login screen, no "create account" popup. Just the tool. That's the point.

2. Enter a Website URL

In the input box, type any website URL. For your first time, just use "youtube.com" - can't go wrong with that.

Why do you need to enter a URL?
Because when someone clicks your tracking link, they need to end up somewhere. The URL you enter is where they'll go after the IP is captured.

Think of it like: Click tracking link → IP gets recorded → Person gets sent to the URL you chose

3. Click "Create Tracking Link"

Hit the button. Wait like 2 seconds. Done.

You'll get two things:

  • Tracking Link - Send this to the person you want to track
  • Results Link - Use this to see who clicked

Important: Save both links! Screenshot them, bookmark them, email them to yourself - whatever. You'll need them later.

4. Send the Tracking Link

Copy the tracking link and send it to whoever you want to track.

Beginner Mistake: Don't just send "click this link" out of nowhere. That's weird and suspicious.

Better approach: Give context. Examples:
  • "Hey, check out this video I found"
  • "Is this link working for you?"
  • "Look at this meme lol"
Make it natural. Pretend the link is actually something you're sharing, not a tracking tool.

5. Wait for Them to Click

This is the part where you can't do anything. You just wait.

They might click right away, might click in an hour, might never click. If they don't click after a day or two, you can try sending another link with better context.

6. Check Your Results

When they click, open your results link (the one you saved earlier). You'll see:

  • Their IP address
  • What city/country they're in
  • What device they used (iPhone, Android, laptop, etc.)
  • What time they clicked
  • Their internet provider

If multiple people click the same link, you'll see all of them listed separately.

Tips for Beginners

Do:
  • Save your results link somewhere safe
  • Make your tracking links seem natural
  • Test with yourself first to see how it works
  • Be patient - they might not click immediately
Don't:
  • Send obvious tracking links
  • Expect exact home address (not possible)
  • Confront someone immediately with the info
  • Share your results link with others
