Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Go to IPLogger Website

Open your web browser and go to iplogger.icu

Example: Type "iplogger.icu" in your browser's address bar and press Enter

2

Enter Any Website URL

In the big text box, paste any website URL. This could be:

  • A YouTube video link
  • A news article
  • A funny meme or image
  • Any website you think they'd be interested in

Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

3

Click "Get IPLogger Link"

After pasting the URL, click the blue button that says "Get IPLogger Link"

Click the button to generate your tracking link

4

Copy Your Special Tracking Link

IPLogger will give you a new link that looks like: iplogger.icu/1AbC2DeF

Copy this link - this is your "tracker link"

Tip: The link will look normal to the person clicking it, but it will track their information!

5

Send the Link to the Person

Send your tracking link to the person whose location you want to find. You can send it through:

  • WhatsApp
  • Facebook Messenger
  • Telegram
  • Email
  • Instagram DM
  • Text message

Example message: "Hey! Check out this funny video I found: iplogger.icu/1AbC2DeF"

6

Wait for Them to Click

When the person clicks your link, they'll be redirected to the original website (like YouTube), but IPLogger will secretly record their information.

They won't notice anything! The process is completely invisible to them.

7

Check the Results

Go back to IPLogger and click "View Logger" next to your link to see:

  • Their exact location (city, country)
  • Their IP address
  • What device they're using (iPhone, Android, Computer)
  • What browser they're using (Chrome, Safari, etc.)
  • Exact time when they clicked
