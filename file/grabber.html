How Does an IP Grabber Work?

An IP grabber works by embedding a tracking link into your content, such as a website, email, or social media post. When someone clicks on the link, their IP address is captured, along with additional information such as the device they are using and their geographical location. All the data is securely logged and can be accessed from a dashboard provided by the service.
