What's Email Tracking?

Email tracking is basically a way to know when someone opens your email. You can also see stuff like their location, what device they used, and when exactly they read it.

This page has all our email tracking guides - different languages, different platforms, different situations. Pick whatever fits what you need.

How Email Tracking Works
The Technology Behind It

Email tracking works by embedding a tiny, invisible image (tracking pixel) in your email. When the recipient opens the email, their email client loads this image from our server, which records the open event along with their IP address and device information.
